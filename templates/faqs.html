Frequently Asked Questions

What types of images can I upload?

You can upload any image of food in common formats such as JPEG, PNG, or GIF. Ensure that the image is clear and well-lit for the best results.

How accurate are the generated recipes?

The accuracy of the generated recipes depends on the quality and clarity of the uploaded image. Our machine learning model is designed to provide the best possible results, but some variations may occur.

Can I use the recipe generator for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use the recipe generator for personal and commercial purposes. However, please ensure that you comply with any relevant copyright or trademark laws when using the recipes generated.

What should I do if I encounter issues with the website?

If you experience any issues with the website or have questions not covered in the FAQs, please contact our support team via the Contact page for assistance.
